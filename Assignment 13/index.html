Taco Vs Ice Cream

Tacos are Balanced You can get almost every nutrient from Tacos. You can eat veggies, and you can get protein from chicken or beef Tacos. You can get grains from the shell and fats from the cheese. And spices complete them to make your craving of the tasty and balanced food for your meal.
Not only does ice cream contain vitamins, it provides lots of energy. Ice cream is rich in carbohydrates, fats and proteins, which are all needed for our bodies to produce energy However, remember to eat in moderation, as too much of a good thing can also have undesired effects.
